Tumbang Preso

Tumbang preso is one of the most well-known traditional Filipino games traditionally played by children on the street. In "Laro Tayo!", players must answer questions to gain points. If their answer is correct, the can will fall; if not, it will shake. If their answers are incorrect, they still have two chances to answer correctly, as long as they have slippers or life.
